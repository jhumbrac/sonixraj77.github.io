The Scalp Massager

Releive aches and pain
Increase blood circulation
The scalp massager will send tingling sensations from scalp to toes and transport you to a more relaxing place.
The custom fit fingers will bend to fit your head. Gently!!!!!
This is the description of the inexpensive extremely functional head massager. I use it and I love it. It uses no batteries or electrical connection and yet is very effective. The design looks like a hand massaging the scalp from the top, the design says what its function is. It simple, light, handy anb can be carried anywhere. A simple design serving its function easily and seamlessly a mark of a good design.

"Ando's architectural style is said to create a "haiku" effect, emphasizing nothingness and empty space to represent the beauty of simplicity. He favors designing complex (yet beautifully simple) spatial circulation while maintaining the appearance of simplicity" - Wikipedia



"The simplicity of his architecture emphasizes the concept of sensation and physical experiences, mainly influenced by the Japanese culture. The religious term Zen, focuses on the concept of simplicity and concentrates on inner feeling rather than outward appearance" - Wikipedia


The images above show how a simple and clean looking design can create such profound feelings about being in a well designed space. The principles of similarity, continuation, proximity, figure and ground are clearly evident in the designs. The designs are beautiful yet simple looking. The material used further enhances the simplicity and a rawness about the spaces created.

Design of the iphone


The iphone, one of the most popular products in the U.S. right now. The reason for its popularity in my opinion is the simplicity and everything on it being self explainatory. The iphone can be used by a toddler to play games or watch videos and even by a senior person not well versed with today's technology. iphones have changed the way people use phones. I am an iphone user and like the ease of using it plus its a great looking product. The product comes with few buttons, the screen changes orientation when the phone is turned around. These features definately exist in the newer phones, however Apple being the pioneers in this new way of using phones has already got their share of the market and now iphone users find it hard to switch to another product. Apple pay is easy to use and accepted at most retailers, which is a wonderful feature of the iphone. The iphone is not just a phone but is like a complete package. Apple provides a great customer service at the "cool" looking apple stores. When there is a problem with the product we have a place to go to. Where does one go for an Android? Iphones as the months go by is getting connected to more and more gizmos to control them eg; the light controls, remote controls etc. The feature of parents having greater control over what their kids use is under the family sharing plan which makes life easy for busy parents. The software is repeadtedly updated for the iphones to work on an upgraded version. The camera is clicks one of the finest pictures that can even be edited without any app or software and there are quite a few features under the edit button as well. All in all this product called iphone has made using phones easy and has enhanced the quality of our lives while doing so!!

Panera Bread App

Panera Bread app as a interactive prototype

Panera bread app works seamlessly and the food can be picked up right from the shelf of the restaurant location picked, without having to interact with any restaurant personels. The app itself has a few buttons and signing up is not neccesary.
You pick your location, pick the dish you want to order, pay with "apple pay" and you are DONE !!!
The order that you pick can be customized and it clearly shows the calories added or removed so you can order at the precise calorie intake you want for the day. While ordering the flow is very smooth. I like it for how quickly I can order food that is healthy and at the amount of calories I can intake for the day.
